# Considerations from Other Sample Agreements

A) Topics handled in `drafts/agreement.md`
- Parties, premises, legal description, and included personal property
- Lease term, renewal, and written notice requirements (including rent increase notice for month-to-month)
- Rent amount, due date, payment location, time-is-of-the-essence, and prorations
- Late charges, returned check fees, and certified-funds requirements
- Application of funds to non-rent obligations before rent
- Security deposit payment, no-interest rule, refund timing, and deduction categories
- Utilities responsibility, required continuous services, and service availability notice
- Use and occupancy limits, permitted occupants, and guest restrictions
- HOA rules compliance, fines reimbursement, and common-area fee responsibility
- Prohibited uses (illegal activity, business use, nuisance, smoking, pool ban, liens)
- Tenant contact info updates
- Pets policy and consent requirement
- Maintenance and condition duties, yard maintenance, pest control, and move-in condition form
- Repair notice duties, tenant-caused damage responsibility, and advance payment for tenant repairs
- Alterations/improvements consent and restoration
- Parking/vehicle limits and towing of inoperable or unregistered vehicles
- Security devices, rekeying requests, smoke detector testing/batteries, and light bulb replacement
- Landlord access, signage, keybox, and trip charges
- Delivery of possession, delayed occupancy remedies, and rent abatement
- Loss/liability allocation, renter's insurance encouragement, and indemnification
- Casualty loss/condemnation rights
- Default, remedies, and mitigation
- Surrender, holdover rent, normal wear and tear, and abandoned property handling
- Landlord's lien
- Assignment/subletting and statutory early termination rights
- Termination in event of sale
- Attorney's fees, representations, and misrepresentation default
- Notice requirements and tenant signature rules for notice to vacate
- Subordination, entire agreement, severability, no waiver, and joint/several liability
- Addenda/disclosures list and information notices (credit reporting, tenant-death contact, sex offender registry, service availability)
- Additional terms (water leak reporting, utilities on, roof/attic ban, lockout charges, HVAC filter neglect)
- Governing law

B) Topics handled in `docs/residential_lease_agreement_max_becca.md` only
- Room reservation for specific individuals

C) Topics handled in `docs/osborne_properties_lease_template.md` only
- Security deposit refund conditioned on advance notice to surrender (60-day notice requirement)
- Tenant liability for bad-faith withholding of the last month's rent (statutory damages)
- Tenant statutory repair remedies and repair-and-deduct procedures
- Entry rights when the premises appear abandoned
- Unauthorized pet penalties, removal, and extermination/cleaning costs
- Payment method restrictions (no cash or one-check requirements)
- Use-violation fines
- Prohibition on waterbeds or liquid-filled furniture
- Vehicle repair restrictions (no long-term repairs or only in enclosed garage)
- Mailbox/postmark not treated as rent receipt for late charges

D) Topics handled in the "sulawesi house" agreement only (`docs/tayloe_house_lease.md`)
- Security deposit refund conditioned on advance notice to surrender (30-day notice requirement)
- Tenant liability for bad-faith withholding of the last month's rent (statutory damages)
- Tenant statutory repair remedies and repair-and-deduct procedures
- Entry rights when the premises appear abandoned
- Unauthorized pet penalties, removal, and extermination/cleaning costs
- Payment method restrictions (no cash or one-check requirements)
- Prohibition on spas, hot tubs, trampolines, and similar items
- Monthly landlord inspection schedule for leaks, filters, and condition
- Month-to-month termination effective-date rules and proration limits
- Replacement-tenant/early termination request process and related fees
